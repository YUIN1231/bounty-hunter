# Hyperbloom Summer Hackathon — Submission

## Project Name
Bounty Hunter — Democratizing Access to Prize Opportunities

## Tagline
Making it possible for any developer — regardless of network or location — to discover and compete for prize money.

## Social good angle
Hackathon opportunities are often discovered through insider networks, mailing lists, and Twitter algorithms. Developers in underrepresented communities, non-English-speaking countries, or without strong tech networks miss out on thousands of dollars in prizes simply due to lack of visibility.

Bounty Hunter is infrastructure for equitable opportunity discovery.

## What it does
- Continuously scans 5+ global platforms
- Surfaces opportunities with AI-ranked priority
- Filter by online participation (accessible regardless of geography)
- Zero cost to use — open source

## Educational impact
- Solo developers learn: what kinds of projects win
- Students see: what platforms exist for prize-based income
- Communities gain: shared intelligence about opportunities

## Tech
Next.js · Claude AI · Open APIs · Tailwind CSS
