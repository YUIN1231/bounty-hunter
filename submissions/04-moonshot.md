# Moonshot Hackathon — Submission

## Project Name
Bounty Hunter — AI-Powered Prize Intelligence

## Tagline
The moonshot: make it financially viable for any solo developer to hunt prize money full-time.

## The big idea
Millions of dollars in hackathon and bounty prizes go unclaimed or go to well-connected insiders who hear about opportunities first. Bounty Hunter democratizes access by automatically surfacing every open opportunity, ranked by AI.

## What it does
- Scans Devpost, HackerOne, Gitcoin, Kaggle, HeroX daily
- Claude AI scores each opportunity: prize value, deadline urgency, solo eligibility, skill match
- Clean dashboard: filter, sort, click-through to apply

## Why it matters
A solo developer with Bounty Hunter running in the background can:
- Never miss a relevant hackathon again
- Focus effort on highest-ROI opportunities
- Build a systematic income stream from prizes

## Moonshot metric
Target: $50,000/year in prizes for a solo developer running this system consistently.

## Tech
Next.js · Claude AI · 5 platform scrapers · Tailwind CSS
