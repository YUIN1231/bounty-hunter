# Open Atlas — AI for Social Good Hackathon — Submission

## Project Name
Bounty Hunter — Equitable Access to Prize-Based Income

## Tagline
AI-powered infrastructure that makes prize money accessible to every developer, everywhere.

## The social problem
The global developer community has millions of talented individuals in low-income countries, rural areas, and underrepresented communities. Hackathon and bounty prizes represent one of the few paths to meaningful income from software skills — but opportunity discovery is deeply unequal.

Developers without strong English networks, Twitter presence, or insider connections miss out on hundreds of thousands of dollars in prizes annually.

## How Bounty Hunter helps
- **Universal access**: scans all major platforms regardless of where the developer lives
- **Language barrier reduction**: AI summarizes opportunities in plain language
- **Filters for online-only**: surfaces remote-accessible opportunities for those who can't travel
- **Free and open**: no paywall, no subscription

## Impact potential
- Reach: any developer with internet access
- Economic: direct income increase for winning developers
- Educational: exposure to global competition standards

## Alignment with Social Good
Closing the information gap in prize-based income is a direct pathway to economic mobility for developers in underserved regions.
